Calligraphy and Handwriting

It is Meditative and relaxing. Creating a calligraphy art piece allows us to move our hands, and putting together word compositions gives us a chance to unleash our creativity.

When we write something meaningful to someone, and when we see that instant reaction from the person, we know that our message has touched them, or made an impact on them. We know that we have connected with the other person. This moment is very powerful, and it is a beautiful moment to behold and treasure.
